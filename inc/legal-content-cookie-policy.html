Cookie Policy — Collective Finity

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function (e.g. keep you logged in) and, where you consent, help us and our partners understand how the Site is used or show relevant advertising. Similar technologies (local storage, pixels) may work the same way and are covered by this policy too.

3. Your choices

  • Essential cookies cannot be disabled, as the Site would not function without them (e.g. you would not be able to stay logged in).
  • Non-essential cookies (advertising, analytics) require your consent for visitors in the EU/UK under the ePrivacy Directive. You will see a cookie consent banner on your first visit where you can accept or decline these, and you can change your choice at any time via the “Cookie Settings” link in the site footer.
  • You can also control or delete cookies through your browser settings — see allaboutcookies.org for browser-specific instructions.

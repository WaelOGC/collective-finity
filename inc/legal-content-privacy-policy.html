2. What personal data we collect

We do not knowingly collect payment card numbers or bank account details — all donations are processed directly by PayPal under PayPal’s Privacy Statement. We never see or store your payment credentials.

We do not use special category data (health, religion, ethnicity, etc.) and ask you not to include such information in any free-text fields (e.g. a bio) on your profile.

2a. Xfinity, engagement & referral data

If you use our Xfinity rewards program, we collect data needed to calculate and verify your points: listening and reading activity timestamps, your Xfinity balance and transaction history, your referral code, and — for a limited anti-fraud window — your IP address at the time a referral is confirmed. This data is used solely to operate the rewards program (calculating balances, detecting abuse, and processing milestone rewards) and is not sold or shared with advertisers.

Xfinity points have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for money. They may be exchanged for gift codes from partner platforms, or — in the future — for benefits on our planned NovaXfinity platform, as described on our Affiliate & Rewards Program page.

3. Why we process your data (legal basis)

Under GDPR Article 6, each processing activity needs a lawful basis:

  • Providing the Service (creating your account, letting you log in, playing music, remembering your preferences, building your playlists) — performance of a contract (our Terms of Service) with you.
  • Keeping the Site secure (activity log, IP address logging, rate limiting, fraud/abuse prevention) — legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) in protecting the Site, our systems, and other users.
  • Sending account-related emails (verification, password reset) — performance of a contract / legitimate interest.
  • Showing advertising — consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) for non-essential cookies used by ad partners where legally required (see our Cookie Policy), otherwise legitimate interest in keeping the Service free to use.
  • Processing your voluntary donation — performance of a contract with PayPal acting on your instruction; we only receive confirmation data, never payment details.
  • Legal compliance — where we must retain or disclose data to comply with a legal obligation (e.g. responding to a valid legal request).

8. Children

Collective Finity is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us at contact@collectivefinity.com and we will investigate and delete it promptly.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Password hashing (we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Secure authentication via trusted, industry-standard OAuth providers
  • Restricted admin access (role-based capabilities)
  • HTTPS encryption for data in transit
  • Activity logging to detect suspicious access patterns

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law (e.g. within 72 hours under GDPR Art. 33, where feasible).

If you nonetheless believe content on the Site infringes your copyright or other intellectual property rights, please notify us using the process below, and we will investigate promptly.

2. How to submit a copyright complaint

Send a written notice to contact@collectivefinity.com with the subject line “Copyright Complaint,” including:

  1. Your name and contact information (email, and mailing address if available)
  2. A description of the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed
  3. The exact URL(s) on Collective Finity where the allegedly infringing content appears
  4. A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  5. A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf
  6. Your physical or electronic signature

3. What happens next

Upon receiving a valid complaint, we will:

  • Review the reported content within 3 business days
  • Remove or disable access to the content if the complaint appears valid
  • Notify the user who uploaded the content, where applicable

4. Counter-notification

If you believe content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice to contact@collectivefinity.com including your contact information, identification of the removed content, and a statement made under penalty of perjury that you have a good-faith belief the content was removed by mistake.

5. Repeat infringers

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate the accounts of users who are found, on separate occasions, to have infringed the intellectual property rights of others.
